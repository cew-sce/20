Welcome to the Green Challenge 2020!

Will your organization be crowned GC's greenest?

The Centre for Greening Government is proud to announce that the Green Challenge is coming back for a 4th year! You and your colleagues are invited to participate from June 1st-5th during Environment Week. Will your branch, sector, region win this year's honours?

About the Green Challenge

Points will be awarded through an on-line tracking system based on participation, knowledge and actions to reduce our carbon footprint and green our activities. This year, the Green Challenge includes five standing challenges which you need to answer (honestly) every day in order to get the points available. On the last day there will be a bonus question, and results will be announced on World Oceans Day (Monday June 8th, 2020). A number of departments and agencies across government are participating, and the organization who collects the most points at the end of the week will win this year's top honours!
